About me

My name is Michel Alexis (pronounced ME-SHELL). In September 2023, I will be a postdoc at the Hausdorff Center for Mathematics working with Christoph Thiele. Before that, I was a postdoc at McMaster University, working with Eric Sawyer. My research interests are in (two weight) weighted norm inequalities for Singular Integral Operators, and Orthogonal Polynomials on the Unit Circle. My PhD advisor was Sergey Denisov.
